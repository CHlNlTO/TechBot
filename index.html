INFORMATION AND COMMUNICATION TECHNOLOGY

Information and Communication Technology (ICT) strand is one of the strands offered in the Technical-Vocational-Livelihood. It is intended to offer you with the knowledge and technical skills in using tools and equipment that allows people to communicate in the digital world.

WHY CHOOSE ICT?

Information and Communication Technology lets you master the basic skills about technology and provides sufficient information that will be added to your basic knowledge which will help you in this digital age.
